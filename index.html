About

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers is dedicated to the advancement of Asian heritage scientists and
engineers in education and employment so that they can achieve their
full career potential.

The Boston University chapter of SASE hosts events with focuses in:
Pre-Professional Advising, Academics & STEM,
Community Service,
and Mentorship!
